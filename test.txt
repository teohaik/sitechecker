The Yellow House (Dutch: Het gele huis), alternatively named The Street 
(Dutch: De straat),[1][2] is an 1888 oil painting by the 19th-century Dutch Post-Impressionist painter Vincent van Gogh.

This title refers to the right wing of the 2 Place Lamartine, Arles, France, 
the house where, on May 1, 1888, Van Gogh rented four rooms.
